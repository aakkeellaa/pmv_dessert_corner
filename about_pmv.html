PMV Dessert Corner is owned by Fatin Audryna, with 35 workers who are all students from the Prime Mover Club at UiTM Machang. Established in 2024 by the Prime Mover Club, PMV Dessert Corner adds value to the college community. Located at the Student Center of UiTM Machang, itâ€™s strategically placed next to a busy pathway that students frequently use, making it the perfect spot for a quick snack. With a motto "CUBA SEKALI AKAN BERKALI-KALI", it offers a delightful variety of desserts to staff and students alike. The space is designed to be both welcoming and convenient for anyone seeking a sweet treat on the go.

VISION

To be the leading dessert provider at UiTM Machang, recognized for exceptional quality, innovation, and service. We are committed to using premium ingredients, maintaining high food standards, and empowering students through hands-on business experience, creating a lasting impact on the campus community.

MISSION

At PMV Dessert Corner, our mission is to create a warm and inviting space where UiTM Machang students and staff can enjoy high-quality, affordable, and delicious desserts. We are committed to delivering exceptional service through our dedicated student workforce, who put in their time and effort to ensure smooth operations and customer satisfaction. By carefully selecting only the freshest and finest ingredients from trusted suppliers, we strive to maintain the highest standards of food quality and taste. Our goal is not only to serve delightful treats but also to provide a valuable learning experience for our student workers, fostering teamwork, responsibility, and entrepreneurial skills.

OBJECTIVE

  • Giving food quality top priority so that students are happy with what our store has to offer.
  • Deliver the greatest and most effective service possible in terms of both quality and efficiency to protect the company's reputation.
  • Giving consumer requirements and food cleanliness top priority.
  • Optimizing food sales to meet the sales goal set by the business.
